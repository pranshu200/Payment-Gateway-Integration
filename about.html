ABOUT

Hello dear donors! Hope you are all safe and healthy!


We invite you to contribute to the fund raising for the daily wage workers by making a generous donation today. Every rupee you give helps to secure a healthy future for these affected people of our society. The effects of Covid-19 and Lockdown in India on the lives of migrants of India have been very dreadful. We are trying to help these people with the essential needs, food supply and sanitation.


Motive

  • Direct Grain Delivery to workers and their families
  • Availing Hospital Facility to the infected.
  • Mask and Sanitizer distribution

Support our Community

With your support, we are continuing to deliver food grains to migrant workers in need, and gearing up this week to meet an increasing rate of distress calls from across the nation. We have also begun to perform higher outreach across other urgent demand verticals like healthcare and sanitation. Given the rapid spread of Covid-19 in India, we are implementing our strategies keeping in mind social distancing, hygiene, and crowd control best practices. Simultaneously, we have been engaging the state to ensure safe passage and living conditions for migrant workers and families.
